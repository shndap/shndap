# 💫 About Me

I'm a senior at **Sharif University of Technology** with a passion for building intelligent systems that perceive, reason, and interact with the world in more human-like ways. My current interests lie at the intersection of **Deep Learning**, **Large Language Models (LLMs)**, and **Time-Series Analysis**, where I enjoy exploring both theoretical foundations and practical applications.

Beyond the technical aspects of AI, I'm fascinated by the human side of intelligence—how machines can help us understand and interpret language, expression, music, and art. I enjoy working on projects that connect rigorous mathematical and computational ideas with meaningful real-world experiences.

### Currently Exploring

* 🤖 LLMs, Reasoning, and Agentic Systems
* 📈 Time-Series Forecasting and Representation Learning
* ⚙️ Distributed Machine Learning Systems
* 🎨 Human-Centered AI and Computational Creativity

I'm always excited by opportunities to bridge cutting-edge research with impactful applications and to better understand how intelligent systems can augment human creativity and decision-making.
